Contents
_________________________

Directory structure:
autorun.exe, autorun.dat, autorun.inf (files for automatically opening the open file)
licence_agreement_e.txt (end-user agreement in English)
Readme.txt (English version of this file)
of_6550.pdf (open file)
Appendices\OpenFile6550-Appendix1.pdf (Wholerock sample element abundance distribution plots)
Appendices\OpenFile6550-Appendix2.pdf (Core box and whole rock geochemmistry subsample photographs, and polished section photomicrographs)
Index\OpenFile_6550.pdx, Index\OpenFile6550\index.idx, Index\OpenFile6550\index1.idx (index files)
Tables\OpenFile6550-Tables1&2.xls (Table 1. Summary of sample metadata information, including DDH location, sampling depth, sample descriptions, and geochemical method key.)
